About CopyHistory

CopyHistory is a native macOS clipboard history manager. It records what you copy, lets you search and organize it (pins, favorites, notes, and custom lists), and paste back quickly — with everything stored locally on your Mac.

Key Features

  • 📋 Automatic clipboard history — text, images, files, and other supported types
  • 🔍 Fast search, including regex where enabled
  • 📌 Pin slots, favorites, per-item notes, and custom lists
  • 👁️ Quick Look and previews for files and rich content
  • ⌨️ Keyboard-first workflow; customizable shortcuts and optional double-tap modifier to open the window
  • 🌍 Multiple interface languages (including English and Chinese, plus additional locales)
  • 🔐 Privacy-first — no analytics or cloud sync; data stays on your device (see Privacy Policy)
