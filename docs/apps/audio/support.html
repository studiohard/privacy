Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What does Audio Converter do?
A: Audio Converter processes local audio files you choose, including supported encrypted formats, and exports them into standard playable formats.
Q: Does Audio Converter upload my files?
A: No. Audio Converter performs conversion locally and does not upload your files or converted output. See the Privacy Policy for details.
Q: Which formats are supported?
A: The app focuses on local conversion and decryption workflows for supported encrypted audio formats together with common audio and video outputs handled by the current version.
Q: Why can a conversion fail?
A: Common causes include an invalid or damaged source file, an unsupported variant, insufficient write permissions for the output folder, or missing metadata required by a specific file format.
Q: Where are converted files saved?
A: Converted files are saved to the output folder you choose in the app. You remain in control of where processed files are written.

Reporting Issues

  1. Describe the problem clearly.
  2. Include the input file type and approximate file size.
  3. List the steps to reproduce the issue.
  4. Include your macOS version and app version.
  5. Attach a screenshot or error message if it helps explain the issue.
