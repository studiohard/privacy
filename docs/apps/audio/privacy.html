Audio Converter Privacy Policy

Audio Converter is a macOS app for converting and decrypting supported local audio files. This privacy policy explains how Audio Converter handles your information.

Last Updated: June 6, 2026

Overview

Audio Converter is a macOS app for converting and decrypting supported local audio files. This privacy policy explains how Audio Converter handles your information.

Data Collection

Audio Converter does NOT collect, transmit, or share your personal data.

What Audio Converter Accesses

  • Files you choose to process: Audio Converter reads the local files you import so it can convert or decrypt them on your Mac.
  • Output folders you choose: The app writes converted files only to locations you explicitly select.
  • App preferences and conversion history: The app may store local settings such as output preferences, metadata completion options, and recent workflow history.

What Audio Converter Does Not Do

  • It does not upload your source files, converted files, file names, or folder contents to any server.
  • It does not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or cross-app tracking technologies.
  • It does not require an account or sign-in to convert local files.

Permissions and Access

Audio Converter only processes files you explicitly provide through file selection or drag and drop, and it only writes results to folders you authorize. File access is limited by macOS permissions and sandbox rules.

Local Storage

Audio Converter stores settings and workflow data locally on your Mac. This can include output preferences, supported processing options, and conversion history that helps the app continue your workflow.

  • Preferences: output settings, workflow options, and related local app configuration
  • History or temporary processing data: local records or temporary files used to complete conversions and help you locate past outputs

Network Use

The app's core conversion and decryption workflows are designed to run locally. If you open external links such as support, privacy, or terms pages, those requests are handled by your browser or system, not by Audio Converter itself.

Data Retention

  • Local preferences and history remain on your Mac until you remove them or uninstall the app.
  • Converted files remain in the output folder you selected until you move or delete them.
  • The app does not keep a remote copy of your files or conversion records.

Children's Privacy

Audio Converter does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be indicated by updating the Last Updated date at the top of this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at huag9998@gmail.com.

Your Control

Because Audio Converter processes data locally on your Mac:

  • You choose which files are imported.
  • You choose where converted results are saved.
  • You can remove the app, local history, and output files at any time.
