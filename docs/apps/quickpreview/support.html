Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What does QuickPreview do?
A: QuickPreview extends macOS Quick Look so you can inspect folder contents, Markdown files, and supported archives more efficiently.
Q: Does QuickPreview upload my files?
A: No. QuickPreview reads selected content locally to generate previews and does not upload that content to external servers. See the Privacy Policy for details.
Q: Which file types are currently supported?
A: QuickPreview currently focuses on folder previews, Markdown previews, and supported archive previews within macOS Quick Look.
Q: Why does a preview sometimes look incomplete?
A: Some previews depend on what macOS allows the Quick Look extension to read for the selected item. Very large folders, unusual file structures, or unsupported formats may lead to limited results.
