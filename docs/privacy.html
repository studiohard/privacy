Privacy Policy隐私政策

CopyHistory is designed around local storage. It does not collect, transmit, sell, or share your personal data.

CopyHistory 以本地存储为核心设计，不收集、不传输、不出售，也不分享你的个人数据。

Overview概览

CopyHistory is a clipboard history manager for macOS. It stores and manages your clipboard history entirely on your local device.

CopyHistory 是一款 macOS 剪贴板历史管理工具。它会在你的本机设备上保存和管理剪贴板历史记录。

Data Collection数据收集

CopyHistory does not collect, transmit, or share personal data.

CopyHistory 不会收集、传输或分享个人数据。

What is stored locally本地保存的内容

  • Clipboard history: copied items such as text, images, files, and other supported pasteboard types.
  • Preferences: settings, shortcuts, and related app configuration.
  • Organization metadata: pins, favorites, notes, and custom list data.
  • 剪贴板历史：复制过的文本、图片、文件以及其他受支持的剪贴板类型。
  • 偏好设置：应用设置、快捷键以及相关配置。
  • 整理信息：置顶、收藏、备注和自定义列表数据。

Storage locations数据存储位置

Released builds have App Sandbox enabled by default. ~ means your home folder.

发布版本默认启用 App Sandbox。~ 表示你的用户主目录。

  • History database:历史数据库： ~/Library/Containers/com.huagx.copyhistory/Data/Library/Application Support/CopyHistory/
  • Preferences:偏好设置： ~/Library/Containers/com.huagx.copyhistory/Data/Library/Preferences/

Rare non-sandboxed developer builds may use ~/Library/Application Support/CopyHistory/ and ~/Library/Preferences/com.huagx.copyhistory.plist. In all cases, data stays on your Mac.

少量非沙盒开发版本可能使用 ~/Library/Application Support/CopyHistory/ 和 ~/Library/Preferences/com.huagx.copyhistory.plist。无论哪种情况，数据都保存在你的 Mac 上。

Permissions权限说明

Accessibility辅助功能

Used only to simulate keyboard events for automatic paste. You can use CopyHistory without this permission if you paste manually.

仅用于模拟键盘事件以执行自动粘贴。如果你选择手动粘贴，可以不授予此权限。

Clipboard access剪贴板访问

Used to read clipboard contents when you copy something, so the item can be stored in local history.

用于在你复制内容时读取剪贴板，并将项目保存到本地历史记录。

Purchases, Services, and Retention购买、服务与保留

Optional Pro subscriptions and lifetime purchases are processed by Apple's App Store. CopyHistory does not collect payment details or Apple ID credentials.

可选的 Pro 订阅和终身购买由 Apple App Store 处理。CopyHistory 不收集付款信息或 Apple ID 凭据。

CopyHistory does not use third-party analytics, advertising, tracking, or cloud sync services. Clipboard history is retained according to your configured app settings and can be cleared from within the app.

CopyHistory 不使用第三方分析、广告、跟踪或云同步服务。剪贴板历史会根据你的应用设置保留，也可以在应用内清理。
