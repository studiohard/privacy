CopyHistory Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 22, 2026

Overview

CopyHistory is a clipboard history manager for macOS. It stores and manages your clipboard history entirely on your local device. This privacy policy explains how CopyHistory handles your information.

Data Collection

CopyHistory does NOT collect, transmit, or share any of your personal data.

What Is Stored Locally

  • Clipboard History: Copied items (text, images, files, and other pasteboard types the app records) are stored locally on your Mac.
  • Application Preferences: Settings and keyboard shortcuts are stored locally (UserDefaults and related app storage).
  • Pins, Favorites, Notes, and Lists: Pin assignments, starred favorites, per-item notes, and custom list/filter metadata are stored in the same local database as your history.

Data Storage Locations

Released builds of CopyHistory have App Sandbox enabled by default. The app only writes inside its sandbox container. ~ means your home folder; you can copy these paths and paste them into Finder → Go → Go to Folder (⇧⌘G) or Terminal.

  • History database (SwiftData): ~/Library/Containers/com.huagx.copyhistory/Data/Library/Application Support/CopyHistory/ — contains Storage.sqlite and related SQLite files.
  • Preferences: ~/Library/Containers/com.huagx.copyhistory/Data/Library/Preferences/ — includes com.huagx.copyhistory.plist and other preference data for the app.

Rare non-sandboxed developer builds may use ~/Library/Application Support/CopyHistory/ and ~/Library/Preferences/com.huagx.copyhistory.plist instead. In all cases, data stays on your Mac.

Permissions Required

CopyHistory requires the following macOS permissions to function:

Accessibility Permission

  • Purpose: Required to enable automatic paste functionality.
  • What it does: Allows the app to simulate keyboard events for pasting.
  • Note: This permission is only used for the paste feature. You can use CopyHistory without granting this permission; you will simply need to paste manually.

Clipboard Access

  • Purpose: To monitor and store your clipboard history.
  • What it does: Reads clipboard contents when you copy something.
  • Note: All clipboard data remains on your device.

In-App Purchases & Subscriptions

CopyHistory offers optional Pro subscriptions and a lifetime purchase through Apple's App Store. All payment processing is handled entirely by Apple. CopyHistory does not collect, store, or have access to any payment information, billing details, or Apple ID credentials.

Third-Party Services

CopyHistory does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No data is sent to any external server.

Data Retention

  • Clipboard history is retained based on your configured retention settings (for example by time or item count), where you enable them.
  • Clear removes history entries but keeps pinned items, favorites, and entries tied to manual custom lists. Clear All removes all stored history entries from the database (see in-app controls).
  • Uninstalling the app does not automatically delete your stored data. To completely remove all data:
    1. Use Clear All in the app (or delete data from within the app) if you want the database emptied first.
    2. Uninstall the app.
    3. Remove the sandbox container and paths listed under "Data Storage Locations" above (for default sandboxed installs, deleting ~/Library/Containers/com.huagx.copyhistory removes container data).

Children's Privacy

CopyHistory does not knowingly collect any information from children. The app is a general-purpose productivity tool suitable for all ages.
