Q: What permissions does the app need?
A: CopyHistory requires the following permissions:
  • Accessibility: Used for the automatic paste feature. You can grant it in System Settings ‚Üí Privacy & Security ‚Üí Accessibility. The app still works without this permission, but you'll need to paste manually.
  • Clipboard Access: Automatically granted. Used to monitor and store clipboard history.
Q: How do I open clipboard history with a keyboard shortcut?
A: The default shortcut is ‚å•V (Option + V). You can customize it in the app's Preferences.
Q: How do I pin frequently used items?
A: In the history window, select the item you want to pin and press ‚åòP. Pinned items appear at the top of the list, with a maximum of 21 pinned items.
Q: Where is my data stored?
A: All data is stored locally on your Mac:
  • Clipboard history: ~/Library/Application Support/CopyHistory/
  • Preferences: ~/Library/Preferences/com.huagx.copyhistory.plist
No data is uploaded to any server.
Q: How do I clear my history?
A: In the app menu, select "Clear" to remove unpinned items. Hold ‚áß (Shift) while clicking "Clear" to remove all items including pinned ones.
Q: What content types are supported?
A: CopyHistory supports:
  • Text
  • Images
  • Files
  • Other clipboard-supported types
Q: How do I search my history?
A: After opening the history window, simply start typing to search. Real-time search and regex search are both supported.
Q: Does the app collect my data?
A: No. CopyHistory fully respects your privacy:
  • All data is stored locally on your device only.
  • No data is uploaded to any server.
  • No personal information is collected.
  • No analytics or tracking services are used.
For more details, see our Privacy Policy.
