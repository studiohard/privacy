❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What permissions does the app need?
A: CopyHistory uses the following:
  • Accessibility: Used for automatic paste (simulated key events). Grant it in System Settings → Privacy & Security → Accessibility. Without it, you can still browse history and copy or paste manually.
  • Clipboard: Used to read the pasteboard so new copies can be recorded. Data stays on your device.
For step-by-step Accessibility setup, see Accessibility Permission Guide.
Q: How do I open clipboard history with a keyboard shortcut?
A: The default shortcut is ⌥V (Option + V). You can change it under Preferences → Shortcuts. You can also enable opening the window by double-pressing a modifier key (for example double Option), configured in the same section.
Q: How do I pin frequently used items?
A: In the history window, select the item and use the pin shortcut (default ⌥P, Option + P — customizable in Preferences). You can have up to 21 pinned slots; pin position (top or bottom) is configurable.
Q: Where is my data stored?
A: Everything is stored locally on your Mac. App Sandbox is on by default for released builds; data lives in the app container. Paths use ~ (your home folder); copy and paste into Finder → Go → Go to Folder (⇧⌘G) or Terminal:
  • Database: ~/Library/Containers/com.huagx.copyhistory/Data/Library/Application Support/CopyHistory/
  • Preferences: ~/Library/Containers/com.huagx.copyhistory/Data/Library/Preferences/ (including com.huagx.copyhistory.plist)
Rare non-sandboxed builds may use ~/Library/Application Support/CopyHistory/ and ~/Library/Preferences/com.huagx.copyhistory.plist. Nothing is uploaded to our servers. See the Privacy Policy for details.
Q: How do I clear my history?
A: In the history window footer, Clear removes entries but keeps pinned items, favorites, and items assigned to manual custom lists. Clear All removes every history entry after confirmation. The menu bar Clear Clipboard History action follows the same rules as footer Clear (not Clear All).
Q: What content types are supported?
A: CopyHistory supports:
  • Text
  • Images
  • Files
  • Other clipboard-supported types
Q: How do I search my history?
A: After opening the history window, simply start typing to search. Real-time search and regex search are both supported.
Q: Does the app collect my data?
A: No. CopyHistory fully respects your privacy:
  • All data is stored locally on your device only.
  • No data is uploaded to any server.
  • No personal information is collected.
  • No analytics or tracking services are used.
For more details, see our Privacy Policy.

🐛 Reporting Issues

When reporting an issue, please provide the following information to help us resolve it quickly:

  1. Description: Describe the issue in detail.
  2. Steps to Reproduce: How can the issue be reproduced?
  3. Expected Behavior: What should happen?
  4. Actual Behavior: What actually happened?
  5. System Information:
    • macOS version
    • CopyHistory version (found in "About")
  6. Screenshots or Logs: If available, please attach relevant screenshots or error logs.
Note: Before submitting issues containing sensitive information, please make sure to remove all personal data (such as passwords, personal files, etc.).
