Privacy Policy for CopyHistory

Last Updated: December 28, 2025

Overview

CopyHistory is a clipboard manager for macOS that helps you manage your clipboard history locally on your device.

Data Collection

CopyHistory does NOT collect, transmit, or share any of your data.

What We Store (Locally Only)

  • Clipboard History: All copied items (text, images, files) are stored locally on your Mac in the application support directory
  • Application Preferences: Your settings and configurations are stored locally using macOS UserDefaults
  • Pinned Items: Items you pin are stored locally in the same database

Data Storage Location

All data is stored locally on your device at:

  • ~/Library/Application Support/CopyHistory/
  • ~/Library/Preferences/com.huagx.copyhistory.plist

Permissions Required

CopyHistory requires the following macOS permissions to function:

Accessibility Permission

  • Purpose: Required to enable automatic paste functionality
  • What it does: Allows the app to simulate keyboard events for pasting
  • Note: This is ONLY used for the paste feature. You can use CopyHistory without this permission if you don't need auto-paste

Clipboard Access

  • Purpose: To monitor and store your clipboard history
  • What it does: Reads clipboard contents when you copy something
  • Note: All data stays on your device

Third-Party Services

CopyHistory does NOT use any third-party services, analytics, or tracking.

Data Retention

  • Clipboard history is retained based on your configured size limit (default: 200 items)
  • You can clear your history at any time from the app
  • Uninstalling the app will NOT automatically delete your data. To completely remove all data:
    1. Clear your history from the app
    2. Uninstall the app
    3. Delete the folders mentioned in "Data Storage Location" above

Children's Privacy

CopyHistory does not knowingly collect any information from children. The app is a general-purpose tool suitable for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • You have complete control over your data
  • You can view all stored data in the application
  • You can delete all data at any time
  • No data leaves your device unless you explicitly share it
